Application

The use of Solid can improve services and user experience, while also creating more opportunities to collaborate with chain partners in a GDPR-compliant manner.

It is applicable in various sectors and domains such as municipalities, government agencies, healthcare, retail, media, etc.


Dutch Examples in Preparation

In the Netherlands, we are involved in the following examples in development, preparation or planning.
More information will follow as completion approaches.

Calculating Allowances

Calculating income allowances for citizens often requires various pieces of information. When these data can be shared from a data vault with the relevant agency, a smoother and more user-friendly process is created.

Healthcare

During your life course and in healthcare situations, multiple healthcare providers are often involved. In those healthcare processes, the person is the pivotal point. By placing health data in a data vault, relevant data can easily move along during necessary contacts. Additionally, other sources of information that can be useful for understanding someone's context can be stored in the data vault.

Sharing Diplomas

Sharing your diploma is a common situation when applying for a job or demonstrating in, for example, healthcare that you have the right qualifications. With the help of data vaults, you can share these safely and responsibly.

Organizations and Municipalities

Up to date contact details are useful and necessary for service-providing organizations to be able to get in touch with their customers. This can be easily and conveniently realized through a data vault. This allows more time to be spent on what really matters: providing service.

Collaboration

Do you work at an organization and do you have a concrete interest in working on one of the above or other use cases, possibly in collaboration with chain partners?
Then contact us via: collaborate@solidcommunity.nl
